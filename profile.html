About Me



My name is Ash! Or Ashe, if you're feeling a litle bit spicy. I just came off of what was supposed to be a gap year after highschool that ended up becoming a gap 2 and a half years. It took some time to think about what I wanted to do with my life, but eventually I made up my mind and decided to enroll at DevMountain and learn how to build websites!



I worked customer service for almost 4 years before coming to DevMountain, so I have certainly had plenty of time to practice my problem-solving skills, (and problem prevention if I could help it...). I went to highschool at a little charter school called "Metropolitan Institute for Arts and Academics", where I mostly studied visual arts. While I wouldn't say I ever got especially good at visual art, I do feel that the skills I learned there will help me with front-end development or web design.



Some of my hobies include:

  • Writing
  • Sewing
  • Playing video games


Although I do aspire to become a web developer, my real ambition is to be able to support my hobby of writing, and I'd love to become a prolific author someday!
